How This Dashboard Works仪表说明

This dashboard is a Nasdaq 100 market-regime classifier. It is built to explain market condition and DCA pacing, not to forecast returns.这个仪表盘是纳指 100 市场状态分类器，用来解释市场环境和定投节奏，不是收益预测。

Inputs指标
  • NDX price and 180-day SMA纳指价格与 180 日均线: Shows trend direction and how far price is above or below its long-term baseline.判断趋势方向，以及价格相对长期基准的偏离程度。
  • VIX / VXN volatilityVIX / VXN 波动率: Measures market stress; high volatility raises low-zone or risk signals depending on price context.衡量市场压力；高波动会结合价格位置影响低位或风险判断。
  • CNN Fear & GreedCNN 恐惧贪婪指数: Tracks sentiment and short-term emotional temperature.衡量市场情绪和短期情绪温度。
  • NDXE / NDX breadthNDXE / NDX 市场广度: Compares equal-weighted Nasdaq 100 with cap-weighted Nasdaq 100 to see whether strength is broad.比较等权纳指与市值加权纳指，观察上涨是否足够广泛。
  • SOX / NDX semiconductor leadershipSOX / NDX 半导体主线强弱: Checks whether the semiconductor core of the tech cycle is confirming or weakening.观察半导体这个科技周期核心主线是在确认还是走弱。
Scoring and output评分与输出
  • Scores评分: The model combines temperature, low-zone, overheat, trend, recovery, and confidence scores.模型合成温度、低位、过热、趋势、修复和置信度评分。
  • Regime状态: Those scores map to regimes such as panic low, stress low, recovery, normal, warm, overheated, and top risk.这些评分会映射为恐慌低位、压力偏低、修复、正常、偏热、过热和顶部风险等状态。
  • Action动作: The action is a DCA pacing reference: add, keep normal, reduce, or pause new buying.动作是定投节奏参考：加仓、正常定投、降速或暂停新买入。
Backtest validation回测验证

  • Walk-forward windows were checked across 2011-2015, 2016-2020, 2021-2026, and multiple stress periods.已在 2011-2015、2016-2020、2021-2026 以及多个压力时期做 walk-forward 校验。

This is a regime and DCA pacing reference, not a return forecast.这是市场状态与定投节奏参考，不是收益预测。
